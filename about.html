Who We Are

A Boutique Firm Built for True Partnership

SENS-SIG is a strategic representation firm specializing in high-stakes market entry and business development within Israel's most demanding sectors: Telecom, Banking, and Critical Infrastructure.

We provide global technology vendors with more than just a "foot in the door." We work alongside them, keeping vendors informed, aligned, and actively involved at every stage of the market entry journey.

In fields like Zero-Hardware Security, AI Analytics, and Enterprise VMS, a sales pitch is never enough. The decision-makers we work with - CTOs, CISOs, procurement leads, require a partner who understands the technology as deeply as they do. That's why we operate as a seamless extension of your team, not just as your local agent.

"We don't operate on your behalf, we work beside you. Every meeting, every negotiation, every integration milestone."
